Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to connect business owners in Littlefield with other businesses as a way of advertising and networking. A Chamber of Commerce can help grow the businesses within a town and grow a sense of community among its city residence.Littlefield has a lot to offer new residents, and the chamber of commerce helps make these opportunities readily available to the city. In this increasingly digital world, it is vital that the Chamber of Commerce as an online presence.
