Target Audience

Personas 1

Fictional Name:

Mike Haywood

Demographics:

Male, married, father of four children, has a MBA from BYU.

Job Title:

Local grocery store manager

Responsibilities:

Managing employees, checking/ordering inventory, working with customers, and in charge of day to day operations.

Goals and Tasks:

Team up with other local businesses for various events, look for ways to support the local community, and stay up to do with community events.

Quote

"My store is an extension of me, so when I designed my store I wanted people to feel that they were in my home."

Personas 2

Fictional Name:

Sadie Atwood

Demographics:

Female, married, mother of 2 children, high school graduated.

Job Title:

Stay at home mom

Responsibilities:

Gets her two young kids ready for school, takes care of her house, does household chores, looks at current community events.

Goals and Tasks:

Wants to stay better informed at current events going on through the community, she wants to get her kids invloved in local acativites, and she would like to know where she could volunteer more in her community.

Quote

"My favorite people call me mom"

Scenarios

  1. A family is thinking about moving into the area and they want to know what activity's there are to do. Also, they want to know what the local school are like, and if the town is family orientated.
  2. A local resident wants is looking for volunteer work and wants to know how he can help out in the community.
  3. A single mother wants to know if there are any local food banks she could receive help from.
  4. A local elementary school wants to do a winter coat donation program, and want to get in touch with local business to help
  5. A individual who just opened a local clothing store is wanting to advertise there business, they look on the chamber of commerce site and start networking with other local businesses.
