Hick's Law

Glassesusa

GlassesUSA

Hick's Law is the principle which states that the more options that are available to a person the longer it will take for them to make a decision. This website which sells eye glasses is a perfect example of this. There are dozens of pages just like this one that shows endless amount of options for a customer to choose from.
